Carbonara

What is Carbonara?
Carbonara is a web platform designed for reverse engineers and malware analysts to improve their work.

Retrieve info about
procedures

Upload your .idb or binary file and retrieve useful comments and tips on every single procedure. Oh, and don't forget to include yours when uploading.

Speed up your
analysis

Does your executable file contain code snippets from other binaries? Well, now through Carbonara you can untangle the puzzle and easily recognize what every procedure does.

Connect with other
reverse engineers

If you need some help during your reversing process, you can connect with colleagues all around the world and instantly share or inquire about information.
