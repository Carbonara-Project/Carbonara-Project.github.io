What is Carbonara?

Carbonara is a web platform designed for reverse engineers and malware analysts to improve their work.

 Upload your .idb or binary file and retrieve useful comments and tips on every single procedure. Oh, and don't forget to include yours when uploading. flash_on
Speed up your analysis
 Does your executable file contain functions from other binaries? Well, now through Carbonara you can untangle the puzzle and easily recognize what every procedure does. people

 If you need some help during your reversing process, you can connect with colleagues all around the world and instantly share or inquire about information.
